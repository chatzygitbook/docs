Use this feature to train your AI Agent using content from your website or sitemap.

**Crawl Methods:**
1. Full Website – Add the homepage and auto-discover all public pages
2. Sitemap – Upload an XML sitemap
3. Manual Links – Input specific URLs

**Grouping & Preview**
- Crawled links are grouped under each homepage
- Click a group to see all fetched URLs and preview content

**Edit & Exclude:**
- Update paths or re-crawl anytime

- Exclude specific pages if needed

**Link Management**
- Delete individual links or entire domain groups
- Bulk delete supported
