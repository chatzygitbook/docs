You can train your Chatzy AI Agent using a variety of content types:
- Text Snippets – Paste plain text directly into the platform.
- Files – Upload documents in formats like `.pdf`, `.docx`, `.txt`, or `.csv` (maximum file size: 20MB).
- Web URLs – Add a website link, and Chatzy will automatically extract relevant content.
- Custom Q&A – Manually enter question-answer pairs to improve precision for expected queries.
- Google Drive – Connect a public folder with view-only access to sync its documents into your agent’s knowledge base.
