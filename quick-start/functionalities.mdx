# ChatzyAI – Core Functionalities

## Always-On Engagement  
AI agents that engage, qualify, and support customers 24/7 across multiple channels—never miss a lead or query.

---

## Omnichannel Presence  
Deploy once, scale everywhere:  
- Website & mobile app  
- WhatsApp, Instagram, Messenger, Telegram, LinkedIn  

---

## No-Code Builder  
Launch custom AI agents with drag-and-drop flows.  
Reusable templates for FAQs, lead capture, and appointment booking—no engineering needed.

---

## Personalization & Context  
AI remembers past interactions, adapts tone, and pulls real-time data (pricing, orders, availability) to give relevant answers.

---

## Secure & Scalable  
Enterprise-grade security, multilingual support, and CRM integrations (Salesforce, HubSpot, Zendesk, MS Dynamics).  
Built to handle traffic spikes without extra headcount.
