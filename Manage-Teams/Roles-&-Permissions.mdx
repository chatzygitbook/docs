
| Role        | Permissions                                                                 |
|------------|------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| Super Admin | Full access to everything. Cannot be removed by any other member.           |
| Admin       | Manage everything except removing Super Admins.                             |
| Editor      | Manage bots, conversations, sources, and campaigns. No billing or team settings. |
| Viewer      | Read-only access to dashboards, campaigns, and contacts. No edits allowed.  |
| Agent       | Can only access and respond to conversations. No access to other data or settings. |
