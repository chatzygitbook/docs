- Go to the **My Team Members** section to invite others.
- Assign their role (Admin, Agent, Viewer, etc.) during invitation.
- Invitations expire after 24 hours if not accepted.

Invitation statuses:
- ✅ **Accepted** – The user has joined the team.
- ❌ **Rejected** – The invite was declined.
- ⏳ **Pending** – The invite hasn’t been acted on yet.

You can *resend* invitations if needed.
