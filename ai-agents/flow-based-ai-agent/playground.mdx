The **Playground** is your testing ground to experiment, tweak, and fine-tune how your AI responds  all in real time.  
See the impact of every change instantly and shape conversations with precision.  

### What you can do in the Playground:
- Simulate real chat scenarios – test how your AI handles different queries.  
- Experiment with system prompts – try variations and find the best tone.  
- Adjust model settings live – temperature, model choice, and more.  
- Compare setups side-by-side – analyze which prompt + model combination performs best.  

A perfect space to train, test, and refine your AI before going live.
