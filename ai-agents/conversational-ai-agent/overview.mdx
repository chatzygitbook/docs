### What It Conversational AI Agent
A natural-language agent that understands typed questions, keeps context, and answers within your approved knowledge. Best for unstructured, help-style conversations.

### Best For
- Customer support (FAQ and troubleshooting)
- Product discovery and comparisons
- Sales Q&A and objection handling
- Knowledge base search across large content

### Key Strengths
- **Understands Language:** Handles varied phrasing and follow-ups.
- **Context-Aware:** Remembers prior turns for coherent threads.
- **Scalable Coverage:** Answers long-tail questions without new flows.
- **Human-Like Tone:** Configurable persona and style.

### Limitations
- **Less Deterministic:** Requires guardrails and clear boundaries.
- **Content Quality Matters:** Needs up-to-date, well-scoped sources.
- **Review & Governance:** Monitor answers and refine allowed scope.

### Setup Notes in ChatzyAI
- Add/curate knowledge sources; define scope and tone.
- Configure guardrails to keep answers within domain.
- Connect channels (e.g., WhatsApp, website) and route to the Inbox.
- Track unresolved intents to improve coverage over time.
