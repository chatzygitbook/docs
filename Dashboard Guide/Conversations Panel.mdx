This is where you can view all conversations users have had with your AI agent.

**Features:**

- **Date Filter**  
  Select a date range (e.g., `"2025-07-13 to 2025-07-20"`) to narrow down chats within a specific time period.

- **Conversation List**  
  Shows a scrollable list of all conversations, sorted by most recent.  
  Each entry includes:  
  - Total number of messages (e.g., `# Msgs: 3`)  
  - Timestamp of the interaction (e.g., `16 Jul '25 at 4:39 PM`)

- **Select a Conversation**  
  Click on any conversation to view its full message history and details.
