Configure how and when your team gets notified about incoming leads.

**Features:**

- **Receive New Leads on Email (Toggle)**  
  Turns email notifications ON or OFF for team members (except those with the Agent role).

- **Lead Qualification Filter (Input Field)**  
  Enter rules/conditions to decide which leads should trigger an email notification.  
  *(Only active when email notifications are ON.)*

- **Notification Frequency (Dropdown)**  
  Control how often emails are sent.  
  Options may include: Every 5 mins, Every 30 mins, Hourly, etc.

- **Extract Entities (Checkbox)**  
  If enabled, the system will automatically extract relevant information like name, company, phone, etc. from user messages.

- **Save Button**  
  Applies and saves your notification settings.
