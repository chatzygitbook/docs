# Welcome to Chatzy\!

![7902f29c 69fd 446f Ac06 6922c2e5cb5b Jp](/images/7902f29c-69fd-446f-ac06-6922c2e5cb5b.jpg)

**Chatzy** is an AI-powered conversational engagement platform that helps businesses automate sales, support, and marketing across multiple digital channels — like websites, WhatsApp, and more. Whether you're a startup or a large enterprise, Chatzy enables you to build and deploy AI agents with **no coding required**.

## 🚀 What Can Users Do on Chatzy?

- 🛠️ **Create and train AI agents** using documents, URLs, FAQs, and more.
- 💬 **Deploy bots** on websites and WhatsApp with no code.
- 🌍 **Engage users in 60\+ languages** for global support.
- 📈 **Run AI-powered campaigns** with smart targeting and automation.
- ⚙️ **Integrate with your CRM** and backend tools like Shopify, HubSpot, and more.
- 🧠 **Fine-tune responses** with revisable Q&A, bot instructions, and live training.
