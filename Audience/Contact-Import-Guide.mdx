How to upload contacts into Chatzy AI using CSV files

This guide walks you through everything you need to successfully import and organize your contacts in Chatzy AI.

### 1. ⏱️ Introduction

In this guide, you'll learn how to:

* Import contacts into Chatzy AI
* Format your CSV file correctly
* Understand contact structure
* Use lists to keep contacts organized

---

### 2. 📁 Navigate to the Contacts Section

* Go to the **Audience** tab from the left sidebar
* Click on **Contacts**

What you'll see:

* A list of contacts (if already imported)
* If no contacts exist, the list will be empty

---

### 3. 🧑‍💻 Contact Structure

Each contact includes three parts:

> ✅ **Basic Info**

* First Name
* Last Name
* Email
* Phone Number

> 🏷️ **Tags**

Labels used to group contacts (e.g. `Lead` , `VIP`)

> ⚙️ **Attributes**

Custom fields like:

* Gender → Male
* Age → 30
* City → Mumbai

Click on any contact to view all their details.

---

### 4. 📥 Start the Import Process

* Click the "**Import CSV**" button
* You'll be taken to the import page
* Download the **Sample CSV** to see the correct format

---

### 5. 📄 CSV Formatting Guidelines

**Required Fields**

* You must provide **either a phone number or an email** for each contact

**Column Headers (must match exactly)**

* Use the headers from the sample CSV
* Replace the sample data with your actual contact info

**Contact Limit**

* You can upload up to **10,000 contacts** per file

---

### 6. 📞 Formatting Phone Numbers

To avoid errors:

* Include the **country code**
* Don't include a **plus sign (+)**
* Remove **spaces, brackets, or symbols**

**Example (India):**

✅ `919876543210`
❌ `+91-98765 43210` or `(+91) 98765 43210`

---

### 7. ⚙️ Custom Attributes

* Any extra columns in your CSV will be saved as **Attributes**
* Stored as key-value pairs

**Example:**

`gender` → `Female`
`company` → `Acme Corp`

These help personalize messages later on.

---

### 8. 🗂️ Apply Lists to Imported Contacts

After uploading your file and clicking Next, you can assign your contacts to a list.

**Options:**

* **Auto-assign:** If no list is selected, Chatzy AI will auto-create a list named like: `Import — 20 Jul 2025 3:42 PM`
* **Manual assignment:** Select an existing list to organize the new contacts

Click **Finish** to complete the import.

---

### 9. 👀 After Import

* Return to the **Contacts** section
* You'll now see your newly added contacts
* Each one includes:
    * Name, email, phone
    * Any custom attributes
    * Tags (if added)

You can edit any contact directly from the table.

---

### 10. ✅ You're All Set!

You've successfully imported contacts into Chatzy AI.
Your contacts are now ready to be used in:

* Campaigns
* Segments
* Automated journeys
* Lead notifications
